Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 27 March 2026

Application: QBO Bookkeeping Tool

1. Overview

The QBO Bookkeeping Tool ("the Application") is a personal bookkeeping automation tool that integrates with QuickBooks Online. This Privacy Policy explains how the Application handles your data.

2. Data Collection

The Application accesses the following data from your QuickBooks Online account:

  • Transaction details (dates, amounts, vendor names, reference numbers, categories)
  • Expense and purchase records
  • Attachment metadata

The Application also stores receipt files that you upload directly through the Application's interface.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to:

  • Match uploaded receipts to QuickBooks Online transactions
  • Attach receipt files to the corresponding transactions
  • Assign sequential reference numbers to expenses

Your data is never used for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the bookkeeping automation described above.

4. Data Storage and Processing

All data processing occurs locally on your own computer. The Application does not transmit your data to any external servers, cloud services, or third parties. Your QuickBooks Online data and uploaded receipts are stored in a local database and file folder on your machine.

5. Data Sharing

The Application does not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to any third parties. The only external communication is between the Application and Intuit's QuickBooks Online platform, using your own authenticated session, to read and update your own financial records.

6. Data Retention

Your data remains stored locally on your computer for as long as you choose to keep it. You may delete the local database and receipt files at any time.

7. Security

The Application runs entirely on your local machine and does not expose any data to the internet. Security of the data depends on the security of your own computer and QuickBooks Online account. We recommend keeping your operating system up to date and enabling multi-factor authentication on your Intuit account.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your machine, you have full control over it at all times. You can view, export, modify, or delete any data by accessing the local database and files directly.

9. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The most current version will always be available at this URL.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.

12. Governing Law
